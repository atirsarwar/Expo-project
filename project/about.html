Our Story

Founded in 2010, RAAS Campus Korangi has been at the forefront of providing quality education and skill development to the youth of Karachi. Our journey began with a simple vision: to empower underprivileged communities through practical education.

2010

Foundation

RAAS Campus Korangi was established with initial enrollment of 50 students in IT and Business programs.

2014

Expansion

Added new programs in Graphic Design and expanded campus facilities to accommodate growing student population.

2018

Recognition

Received accreditation and recognition for excellence in vocational education and training.

2023

Modernization

Complete digital transformation with state-of-the-art computer labs and online learning platforms.
