Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about admissions and campus life

What are the admission requirements?

Minimum requirement is Matriculation or equivalent. Some programs may have additional requirements. Please check the specific program details for complete information.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Contact our admission office for eligibility criteria and application process.

What are the class timings?

We offer morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM) and evening (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM) shifts for most programs. Specific timings may vary by course.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that helps students with job placements, internships, and career counseling.

Can I pay fees in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible installment plans for all programs. The number of installments varies by program duration.

What facilities are available on campus?

Our campus features modern classrooms, computer labs, library, sports facilities, cafeteria, and student common areas.
