Admission Requirements

General requirements for all programs at RAAS Campus Korangi

Educational Qualification

  • Minimum Matriculation or equivalent
  • Relevant educational background
  • Basic computer literacy

Identification Documents

  • CNIC or B-Form copy
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Domicile certificate (if applicable)

Educational Documents

  • Matric/O-level certificate
  • Intermediate/A-level certificate
  • Previous degree certificates

Additional Requirements

  • Personal interview
  • Basic assessment test
  • Program-specific requirements

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions

What are the admission requirements?

Minimum requirement is Matriculation or equivalent. Some programs may have additional requirements. Please check the specific program details for complete information.

Is there an age limit for admission?

There is no upper age limit. However, students must be at least 16 years old to enroll in our programs.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Contact our admission office for eligibility criteria and application process.

Can I pay fees in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible installment plans for all programs. The number of installments varies by program duration.

What is the admission process timeline?

The admission process typically takes 1-2 weeks from application submission to enrollment confirmation, depending on program availability.
