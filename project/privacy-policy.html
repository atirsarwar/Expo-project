1. Introduction

RAAS Campus Korangi ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

1.1 Scope

This policy applies to:

  • Prospective and current students
  • Parents and guardians
  • Website visitors
  • Donors and supporters
  • Job applicants
  • All other individuals who interact with RAAS Campus Korangi

1.2 Consent

By using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, residential address
  • Demographic Information: Age, gender, nationality, educational background
  • Identification Information: CNIC/B-Form number, passport details
  • Academic Information: Educational records, test scores, course preferences
  • Financial Information: Payment details, fee records, scholarship information
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information

2.2 How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • Online admission forms and applications
  • Website registration and contact forms
  • Email communications and newsletters
  • In-person interactions and interviews
  • Payment processing systems
  • Automated technologies like cookies and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Admission Processing: To evaluate and process your application
  • Academic Services: To provide educational programs and support
  • Communication: To send important updates and information
  • Administrative Purposes: For record-keeping and institutional management
  • Financial Transactions: To process payments and manage accounts
  • Safety and Security: To ensure campus safety and prevent fraud

3.2 Secondary Purposes

We may also use your information for:

  • Institutional research and improvement
  • Marketing and promotional activities (with consent)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Alumni relations and networking

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 When We Share Information

We may share your information with:

  • Educational Authorities: As required by educational regulatory bodies
  • Service Providers: Third parties who provide services on our behalf
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Emergency Situations: To protect vital interests in emergency situations

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We work with trusted third parties including:

  • Payment processors for fee collection
  • IT service providers for system maintenance
  • Marketing agencies for promotional activities
  • Educational partners for collaborative programs

All third-party providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

5. Data Protection and Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure network infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Staff training on data protection

5.2 Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Immediately investigate the incident
  • Take steps to mitigate the breach
  • Notify affected individuals as required by law
  • Report to relevant authorities if necessary

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for targeted advertising (with consent)

6.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookie settings through:

  • Your browser settings
  • Our cookie consent banner
  • Opt-out mechanisms for specific tracking technologies

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (where applicable)
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Data portability (where applicable)

7.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise your rights, please:

  • Submit a written request to our Data Protection Officer
  • Provide sufficient identification for verification
  • Specify the right you wish to exercise

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

8.1 Retention Periods

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including:

  • Student Records: 10 years after course completion
  • Financial Records: 7 years for tax and audit purposes
  • Website Analytics: 2 years from collection
  • Marketing Data: Until consent is withdrawn

8.2 Data Disposal

When data is no longer needed, we securely dispose of it using:

  • Secure deletion methods for digital data
  • Shredding for physical documents
  • Professional data destruction services for sensitive information

9. Children's Privacy

9.1 Protection of Minors

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 without parental consent
  • For students under 16, we require parental consent for data processing
  • We provide special protections for children's data

9.2 Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review their child's personal information
  • Request deletion of their child's information
  • Refuse further collection or use of the information

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

10.1 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:

  • Changes in our practices
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Technological developments
  • Feedback from our community

10.2 Notification of Changes

We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Notices on our website
  • Announcements through our communication channels

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
